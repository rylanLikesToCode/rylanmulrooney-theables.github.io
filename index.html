The Ables

Disclaimer: All ables can communicate and interact with a living species or form that the others cannot communicate with. This is known as their “addilian”. For example, the Faint can see and talk to ghosts.

Notes

Genetics do not contribute to Ables. For example, if two blazes had a child, that child is just as likely to be a glace as a blaze.

Your able is revealed at puberty, and there is no way discovered to predict it beforehand.

There are different clothes stores designed for different Ables, as every able has distinguishing physical features.

Everybody will, when going through puberty, discover a symbol embedded into their skin, right under the palm, on their arms. Every able has a distinguishing symbol, known as a symblim. For example, the Avians have a set of wings.
